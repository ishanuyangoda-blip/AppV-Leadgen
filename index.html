The problem

Most SaaS management tools weren't built for advisors.

They assume one organisation, one IT team, and enterprise-grade infrastructure. Most of your clients have none of that.

01

No multi-client view

Every other tool assumes you work inside one organisation. Switching between client accounts manually isn't a workflow — it's overhead.

02

Discovery requires infrastructure SMBs don't have

Most discovery tools rely on SSO and identity providers. Your clients use Xero and QuickBooks. AppVentory works from what's already there.

03

Dashboards aren't client deliverables

Finding a problem is not the same as presenting it. Your clients need structured findings — not a login to another platform to explore.
