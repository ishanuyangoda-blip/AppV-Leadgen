# AppVentory — Claude Context File

## 1. Overview
AppVentory is a SaaS discovery, spend management, and advisory enablement platform designed to help businesses and advisors gain full visibility into their software ecosystem, reduce waste, and make better technology decisions.

It combines financial data, usage insights, and automation into a single platform to track, optimise, and manage software spend and adoption.

---

## 2. Core Value Proposition

**AppVentory helps businesses and advisors understand, optimise, and act on their software stack — not just track it.**

Unlike traditional SaaS management tools that focus only on visibility, AppVentory connects:
- Spend (via accounting integrations)
- Usage (via SSO/IDP integrations)
- Advisory workflows (for accountants/tech advisors)

---

## 3. Key Capabilities

### 3.1 SaaS Discovery
- Automatically detects software from financial transactions
- Identifies vendors, subscriptions, and spend patterns
- Builds a real-time app inventory

### 3.2 Spend Management
- Tracks recurring costs and renewal cycles
- Identifies unused or underutilised licenses
- Highlights duplicate or overlapping tools

### 3.3 Usage Insights
- Integrates with identity providers (e.g., Google Workspace, Microsoft)
- Surfaces user-level activity and adoption trends
- Flags inactive users and wasted licenses

### 3.4 Renewal Management
- Predicts renewal dates based on transaction patterns
- Sends alerts before renewals
- Enables proactive cost control

### 3.5 Custom Rules & Automation
- Categorise apps automatically
- Flag high-risk or duplicate tools
- Create workflows for optimisation

### 3.6 Reporting & Advisory
- Financial and system reports
- ROI and savings insights
- Enables advisors to present clear recommendations to clients

## 4. Target Markets

AppVentory serves two distinct but connected markets, each with different needs and value drivers:

### 4.1 Advisors (Primary Growth Lever)
**Who:**
- Tech advisors
- Accountants
- Managed service providers (MSPs)

**Core Need:**
Manage multiple client tech stacks efficiently while delivering measurable value.

**What AppVentory Enables:**
- Centralised multi-client visibility across apps, spend, and usage
- Structured advisory workflows (identify, recommend, track outcomes)
- Client-ready reporting (cost savings, overlaps, optimisation progress)
- Scalable service delivery without increasing manual effort

**Outcome:**
Turn SaaS visibility into a repeatable, monetisable advisory service.

---

### 4.2 SMBs (Direct Users)
**Who:**
- Small and medium-sized businesses
- Founders / operators managing their own tech stack
- Finance teams needing cost control

**Core Need:**
Understand where money is going and eliminate waste without complexity.

**What AppVentory Enables:**
- Full visibility of all software and subscriptions in one place
- Clear identification of unused licenses and redundant tools
- Simple renewal tracking and alerts
- Practical insights without needing deep technical expertise

**Outcome:**
Reduce software spend, simplify the stack, and regain control over tools.
